Home Page

Study Buddy is an interactive platform designed for students, educators, and lifelong learners. It enables users to create customized study materials such as practice tests and flashcards. Whether you're preparing for a major exam, teaching a class, or just brushing up on your knowledge, Study Buddy is the perfect tool to enhance your learning process.

You can easily enter questions, answers, and relevant topics to generate personalized practice tests. Or, if you prefer a more hands-on approach, turn that information into flashcards for quick review sessions. With Study Buddy, effective studying is just a few clicks away!
